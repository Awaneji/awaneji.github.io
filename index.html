Simply Softee

Simply Softee was formed upon listening to a program which was aired on the national broadcaster (ZTV) and on a radio station (STAR FM) where the Founding Director of Shamwari Ye Mwanasikana Mrs Ekenia Chifamba was addressing challenges young girls are facing in the urban and rural communities. We therefore came up with this idea of producing re-usable sanitary towels. We then set down as women in the community and came up with the idea of the production of re-usable sanitary wear, since some women have gone to the extent of using old cloth, leaves and some other unhygienic methods. Some school girls even fail to go to attend lessons for some days due to this predicament. The health factor is that most of the disposable sanitary towels which are being thrown everywhere posing a health hazard to the nation as they are washed away into our rivers and dams where we get our drinking water.

About Us

"Simply Softee was formed on the 9th day of June 2017 with the aim of producing affordable, accessible and comfortable re-usable sanitary wear for girls and women in homes, schools and rural and urban communities. Simply Softee is based in Zengeza, Chitungwiza."

Vision

We envision a nation where all women and girls from grassroot level have access to affordable, healthy and comfortable sanitary wear within rural and urban communities.

Mission

Constantly produce and supply durable,healthy and affordable re-usable sanitary wear for girls and women within rural and urban communities.

Goals

  • Produce comfortable re-usable sanitary towels for girls and women.
  • Instill confidence in girls and women.
  • Maintain cleanliness and a healthy environment for the whole nation.

Objectives

  • To promote reproductive health of women by providing safe re-usable sanitary wear.
  • To provide affordable, environmentally friendly, durable and hygienic sanitary towels to girls and women.
  • Providing a home-grown solution for girls and women, a proudly Zimbabwean product
